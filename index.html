About Us

Founded by a principal engineer with over 13 years of experience in structural and civil design, N.T. Allan Engineering Inc delivers practical, high-quality solutions for residential and commercial buildings throughout California. Our design approach ensures every project, from a custom home to a commercial development, is efficient, buildable, and ready for permit approval, with stamped and sealed engineering documents provided for all required submittals.
